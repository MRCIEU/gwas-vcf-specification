GWAS-VCF:
- source and version of summary statistics
- study IDs (e.g., PMID/DOI of publication describing the study, or accession number and repository of individual-level data)
- description of the trait(s) studied
    - type
    - association test used
    - sample size
    - ancestry
    - measurement unit
    - source and version of trait IDs
        - Experimental Factor Ontology
        - Human Phenotyping Ontology
        - Medical Subject Headings IDs for clinical and other traits
        - Ensembl Gene IDs for eQTL datasets
